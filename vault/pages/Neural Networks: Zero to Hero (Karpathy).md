## Summary
Andrej Karpathy's video lecture series building neural networks and GPT-style language models from scratch in Python, starting from micrograd's autodiff engine through to a working GPT implementation with tokenization.

## Key Takeaways
- Implementing backprop from scratch via a scalar autodiff engine (micrograd) builds durable intuition for how gradients flow through any architecture
- Byte-pair encoding tokenization decisions have outsized effects on downstream model behavior and efficiency
- Building GPT from scratch demystifies the architecture — it is a direct, incremental extension of the bigram and MLP language models taught earlier in the series
