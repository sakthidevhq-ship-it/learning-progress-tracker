## Summary
Devlin et al.'s BERT paper introducing bidirectional pre-training for language understanding. Demonstrates masked language modeling and demonstrates state-of-the-art results on diverse NLP benchmarks through fine-tuning.

## Key Takeaways
- Bidirectional pre-training captures richer contextual representations
- Masked language modeling enables learning from unlabeled text
- Pre-train and fine-tune paradigm enables efficient transfer learning
