## Summary
A curriculum module covering the building blocks of neural networks — perceptrons, forward and backward propagation, activation functions, and optimization algorithms like SGD and Adam. Includes worked derivations of backprop through simple architectures.

## Key Takeaways
- Backprop is just the chain rule applied systematically through a computational graph
- Activation function choice affects gradient flow and training stability
- Adam adapts per-parameter learning rates using running moment estimates, converging faster than vanilla SGD in most cases
